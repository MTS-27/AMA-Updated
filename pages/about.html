About AMA

The Association of Muslim Apprentices (AMA) is a grassroots, youth-led organisation dedicated to supporting, representing, and empowering Muslim apprentices across the UK.

Our Mission

Founded in response to the lack of visibility and access for Muslims in the apprenticeship world, AMA exists to challenge stereotypes, open up opportunities, and build a supportive network.

We work closely with employers, community leaders, and government bodies to drive structural change and open new doors for the next generation.
